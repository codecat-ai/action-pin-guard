# Code of Conduct

This project aims to be a practical and respectful place to collaborate.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct, constructive, and specific.
- Assume good intent while still asking for clear evidence on technical claims.
- Keep discussion focused on the project and its users.
- Credit others accurately and respect licenses.
- Accept maintainer decisions about scope, security, and release timing.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or exclusionary conduct.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeatedly derailing issues or pull requests after a maintainer decision.
- Submitting copied code, text, or assets without compatible licensing and clear
  attribution.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns to the maintainers through a private channel if one is
available, or by opening a minimal issue that asks for a maintainer contact.
Maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, or block participation when
needed to protect the project.
